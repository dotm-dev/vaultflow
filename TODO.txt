         - Timezone dropdown in Settings > Regional Settings with 18 IANA

             • Category details time filters (this_month, last_3_months,
               this_year, custom date range)
             • Transaction date labels and detail modal timestamps
             • Manual expense modal live clock
             • Last sync and vault lastSaved timestamps
         - Persists to IndexedDB, syncs to Google Drive, restores on unlock
